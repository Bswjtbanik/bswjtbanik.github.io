Who I am

I'm a Senior iOS Developer based in Dhaka, Bangladesh, currently working at GoZayaan — a travel-tech company serving users across Bangladesh, Singapore, and Malaysia.

I specialize in building secure, scalable mobile experiences — from biometric authentication using Secure Enclave to multi-gateway payment integrations and real-time flight booking systems.

Before GoZayaan, I spent 4 years at CIBL Technology Consultants, leading iOS development for major Bangladeshi banking apps including EBL Skybanking, NRBC Planet, and NBL iPower — serving hundreds of thousands of daily users.

I love clean architecture, performance-first thinking, and shipping apps that just work.
